Events


Spirit of Ramadan

The act of fasting calls for Muslims to practice self-discipline and sacrifice, as well as reflect upon and show compassion for the poor and less fortunate. But there are middle class family and people living below poverty line who are struggling to meet their month ends with the soaring price of basic necessities during this month and yet anyhow break their fast in miserable condition .
So nishonkoch foundation stands beside these group of people with their event ‘ spirit of Ramadan’ in which ration and basic commodity are provided .



Education for All

Education is a fundamental right, and it is also the most powerful tool we have for sustainable development. Investing in a quality education for every child, youth and adult is essential.
Every year Nishonkoch Foundation organizes an event to donate stationaries and numerous educational supplies consisting of pens, colour pencils, copies, erasers and sharpeners at the event titled "Education for All "



Trashtag

Waste management in Bangladesh faces many challenges due to its large, rapidly growing population in a densely populated country. With this population growth, there is an increasing problem of waste management.
'Trash Tag ' is an event held by Nishonkoch foundation in which its volunteers are allocated different region of dhaka city , where city corporation sweepers cannot get to the cleaning process , and spread awareness to keep our environment around clean.



Miscellaneous programs

Throughout the year we also arrange various other programs based on education, women empowerment, hygiene protocol and many another events.
